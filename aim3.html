Terms & conditions

1.Placement. Your song will be added to the 444Music Boomplay playlist and remain featured for the agreed promotional period.

2.Streams. A minimum of 2,500 plays is guaranteed per featured song, generated through organic playlist plays. Plays beyond the guaranteed number are not capped.

3.Promotion. 444Music will create and publish promotional artwork for your feature across its Instagram, TikTok, and X accounts as part of the package.

4.Delivery window. Playlist placement and promotional posts go live within 48 hours of payment and content approval, unless otherwise communicated.

5.Content ownership. You retain full ownership of your music. 444Music is granted a limited license to stream and promote the submitted track for the duration of the feature only.

6.No chart or virality guarantee. While streams are guaranteed, 444Music does not guarantee chart placement, algorithmic playlisting, or viral outcomes on external platforms.

7.Payment. The feature fee is non-refundable once your song has been added to the playlist and promotional content has been published.

8.Conduct. Submitted music must be original or properly licensed. 444Music reserves the right to remove any content found to be in violation of copyright.
