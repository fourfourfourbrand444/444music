Release Payment Agreement

Please read this carefully before continuing.

IMPORTANT: If you selected “Release & Pay Later (GHS 0)”, your music WILL NOT go live on digital stores immediately.

When you choose GHS 0 – Release & Pay Later:

  • Your release will be reviewed and prepared.
  • Your music will be held in pending status.
  • Your release will only be distributed after payment has been made and confirmed successfully.
  • Distribution begins immediately after confirmation.
⚠️ Your release will NOT go live on stores until payment is completed.

If you prefer your music to be distributed immediately after submission, please choose the Paid Single Plan instead.

The Paid Plan confirms payment first, then distributes directly to 100+ stores.
