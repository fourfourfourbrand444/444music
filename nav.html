Questions, answered

What's the difference between Draft & Release and Instant Release?

Both cost the same — GHS 39 / $3 — the only difference is timing. Draft & Release lets you upload now and pay whenever you decide to publish. Instant Release charges upfront so your track skips straight to review with no waiting.

Can I release an EP or full album?

Yes. The EP/Album plan is a flat GHS 69 / $6 for the entire project, no matter how many tracks it contains — one cover, one set of metadata, one release.

Do prices change based on my location?

No. Every plan is shown in both Ghana cedis and US dollars at the same rate, so artists releasing from anywhere pay the same fair price.

Is there a subscription or recurring fee?

Never. You pay once per release. There's no monthly plan, no renewal, and no hidden charge after your track goes live.
