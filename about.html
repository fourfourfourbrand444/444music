About Us

444Music Distribution is a digital music distribution platform dedicated to helping independent artists release their music globally. We provide fast, reliable and affordable delivery of music to all major streaming platforms.

Our mission is to empower creators by giving them full control over their content, earnings, and audience reach. We support upcoming and professional artists with the tools needed to grow in the modern music industry.

We are committed to transparency, secure payments, and artist satisfaction — because your art deserves to be heard everywhere.
