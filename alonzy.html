Frequently Asked Questions

Learn everything about releasing music, royalties, referrals, and managing your account on 444Music Distribution.

How do I upload my music to 444Music Distribution?
+
Log in to your dashboard, create a new release, upload your audio file, cover art, and fill in the required details such as artist name, release date, and genre.
Which stores will my music be sent to?
+
Your music can be delivered to major streaming platforms including Spotify, Apple Music, TikTok, YouTube Music, Audiomack, and many more global stores.
How long does it take for my release to go live?
+
Most releases are processed within 2–5 business days, but some platforms may take longer depending on their approval system.
Can I schedule a future release date?
+
Yes. We recommend scheduling your release at least 7 days in advance to ensure it reaches all platforms on time.
What audio format should I upload?
+
We recommend uploading WAV files (16-bit or 24-bit) with high audio quality for the best distribution results.
Why was my release rejected?
+
This usually happens due to copyright issues, poor audio quality, incorrect metadata, or artwork that doesn't meet store guidelines.
How do I earn money from my music?
+
You earn royalties whenever people stream or download your music on supported platforms. Earnings are collected and shown in your dashboard.
When will I receive my royalties?
+
Streaming platforms usually report earnings every month, but some may take 2–3 months before the first royalties appear.
What is the minimum withdrawal amount?
+
You must reach the minimum payout balance before requesting a withdrawal. You can check this requirement in your earnings dashboard.
Why did my withdrawal fail?
+
Withdrawals may fail if your balance is insufficient or your payment information is incomplete or incorrect.
Can I edit my release after submitting?
+
Yes, but only before the release is delivered to stores. Contact support immediately if changes are required.
Can labels use 444Music Distribution?
+
Yes. Labels can manage multiple artists and releases from a single dashboard.
Is my music ownership protected?
+
Yes. You keep 100% ownership of your music. We only distribute your content to digital stores.
How does the referral program work?
+
Share your referral link with new artists. When they join and release music, you earn referral rewards from their activity.
Where can I find my referral link?
+
Your referral link is available inside your dashboard under the referral or affiliate section.
When do I get paid from referrals?
+
Referral earnings are added to your account once the referred user completes payments or releases through the platform.
