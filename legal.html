About Us

Who We Are

444Music Distribution is an independent artist platform built to close the gap between emerging talent and global audiences. We give you the infrastructure that major labels have — without the gatekeeping.

Founded by music lovers and technology builders, our mission is simple: put artists in control of their careers, their royalties, and their fanbase — completely and without compromise.

Fast Delivery Your music live on Spotify, Apple Music & more within 24–48 hours.
Real-Time Analytics Track streams, saves, and revenue across all platforms in one dashboard.
Rights Protection Content ID and ISRC registration included with every release.
Artist Support Dedicated support team available 7 days a week for all plan tiers.

Our Mission

We believe that great music deserves to be heard — regardless of budget, connections, or geography. 444Music exists to make professional-grade distribution accessible to every independent artist on the planet.

Our platform is built on three pillars: Speed, Transparency, and Fair Compensation. We take nothing from your royalties. Every stream, every playlist add, every download — 100% of your earnings flow back to you.

Privacy Policy

Data We Collect

  • Account registration details — name, email address, and artist profile information.
  • Payment information processed securely via PCI-DSS compliant third-party providers. We never store your card details.
  • Platform usage analytics to improve your experience and optimize our distribution network.
  • Music metadata — title, ISRC, release date, genre — required to distribute your content accurately.

Important: Your data is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for advertising purposes. We collect only what is necessary to operate our service.

Your Rights & Protections

  • Right to Access: Request a full export of your personal data at any time via your account settings.
  • Right to Deletion: Close your account and request permanent deletion of all personal data within 30 days.
  • Right to Correction: Update or correct any inaccurate personal information from your profile dashboard.
  • Security: All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We conduct regular third-party security audits.
  • GDPR & CCPA Compliant: We honour all applicable data protection regulations globally, including for EU and California residents.

Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies necessary for the platform to function, and optional analytics cookies to improve our product. You can manage cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings or our cookie consent panel.

We do not use cross-site advertising trackers or sell browsing data to any third party. By continuing to use 444Music Distribution, you agree to our minimal, privacy-respecting cookie policy.

Terms & Conditions

Artist Eligibility & Content Rules

  • You must hold full copyright ownership or exclusive distribution rights to any content you upload.
  • No copyrighted samples, cover songs (without a licence), or AI-generated content presented as original are permitted.
  • Content violating our community standards — including hate speech, explicit violence, or illegal material — will be removed without notice.
  • Each release must include accurate metadata: correct artist name, title, genre, and release date.

Royalties & Payouts

Rule Detail Status
Royalty Rate 100% of net royalties passed to the artist — no platform cut. Guaranteed
Payout Threshold Minimum balance of $10 USD required to trigger a payout. Standard
Processing Time 3 – 7 working days after withdrawal request is submitted. Variable
Payment Methods Bank transfer, PayPal, and Mobile Money (select regions). Available
Reporting Lag Streaming platforms typically report earnings 45–60 days after the month end. Industry Norm
Account Fraud Artificially inflated streams result in permanent suspension and forfeiture of withheld royalties. Zero Tolerance

Royalties from streaming platforms may take up to 60 days to appear in your 444Music dashboard due to reporting cycles outside our control.

Account Suspension & Termination

  • Accounts found to be using stream-farming services, bots, or any form of artificial engagement will be permanently banned.
  • Repeated copyright infringement violations will result in account suspension and takedown requests filed with all platforms.
  • Artists may voluntarily close their accounts at any time. Pending royalties will be paid out after verification.
  • 444Music reserves the right to update these terms with 30 days' notice via email to registered artists.
